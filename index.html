Code of Conduct

Our code of conduct ensures a positive and inclusive environment for all members:

  • Commitment to learning and growth

About Catalyst Club

The Catalyst Club at Haldia Institute of Pharmacy is a dynamic student organization dedicated to fostering innovation, leadership, and community engagement. Founded with the vision of creating positive change, we provide a platform for students to collaborate, learn, and grow together.

Our Mission

To empower students through innovative learning experiences and collaborative projects.

Our Vision

Creating future leaders and innovators in the field of pharmacy.
